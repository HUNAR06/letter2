My Love,

Seeing you in college every day is the best part of my routine. No matter how hectic life gets, every glance, every moment with you makes everything better.

You are my favorite part of every day.

I catch myself smiling just thinking about you.

I wish every class, every hallway, every stolen moment could last longer. Time with you never feels like enough, but even the shortest moments make my heart race.

Every second with you is a memory I cherish.

You make the ordinary feel magical.

Some days, I just sit and wait for the moment I get to see you again. And when I do, everything feels right. No matter what happens, knowing I’ll see you makes it all worthwhile.

You are my reason to look forward to tomorrow.

You make every day special just by being in it.

Until the next time I see you, just know I’m thinking about you, missing you, and counting down the minutes until we’re together again.

You are my favorite hello and my hardest goodbye.

You are my person, my happiness, my always.

Forever yours.

My love, no matter how far we are, my heart is always with you. You are my forever, my safe place, and my happiest thought. I love you endlessly.
